About Me

Welcome to my personal space! I'm passionate about FPV drone flying and custom electric vehicle builds. My journey started with a simple interest in remote-controlled aircraft and has evolved into designing and building high-performance FPV drones and modifying electric vehicles like my custom Surron.

I enjoy pushing the boundaries of what's possible with modern electric technology, whether it's capturing breathtaking aerial footage or optimizing my Surron for both off-road performance and street use.

When I'm not flying drones or riding, I'm constantly researching new components, techniques, and innovations in the electric vehicle space. This website showcases my builds, projects, and provides links to resources I find valuable.

My Builds

Custom FPV drones and electric motorbike builds

FPV Drone Builds

I specialize in building custom FPV (First Person View) drones optimized for different flight characteristics and applications.

  • 5" Freestyle Quad with DJI Digital FPV System
  • 3" Cinewhoop for indoor cinematography
  • 7" Long Range Explorer with GPS return-to-home
  • Custom 3D printed parts and frames

Each build is carefully tuned for optimal flight characteristics using Betaflight configurator, with custom PID settings and motor configurations.

Surron Electric Motorbike

My custom-built Surron has been modified for enhanced performance both on and off-road.

  • Upgraded controller for increased power output
  • Custom battery configuration for extended range
  • Reinforced suspension for aggressive trail riding
  • Upgraded braking system for better stopping power
  • Street-legal conversion kit with turn signals and headlight

The Surron offers an incredible balance of power, agility, and range, making it perfect for both trail adventures and urban commuting.
